AKALI


About

Akali is another League of Legends character. In the League of Legends universe, Akali is an assassin she uses stealth and other ninja like weapons to her advantage. in the KDA universe Akali is the main rapper/ lead dancer of her group. Akali alongside her group memeber Kaisa has a very built and stable body. She is voice by the Kpop star Soyeon. Akali is known for her childish fun personality in her group.

Voice Actor

Soyeon

Jeon So-yeon (Korean: 전소연; born August 26, 1998), known mononymously as Soyeon, is a South Korean rapper, songwriter, composer and record producer signed to Cube Entertainment. She first gained attention for competing in the television shows Produce 101 and Unpretty Rapstar before debuting as a solo artist on November 5, 2017. On May 2, 2018, she debuted as the leader and main rapper of the girl group (G)I-dle for whom she has written and produced most title songs.She is also part of SM Station X girl group project Station Young, and has portrayed League of Legends character Akali in the virtual musical groups K/DA and True Damage.
